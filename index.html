The Solar Pets

Season two is the introduction of our Solar Pets. It will consist of 5,000 Hatchable eggs, 3,000 of which are reserved for Kalvin/Galaxy Pass owners. The eggs will be hatched through a proof of work system where the owner has to perform actions and take care of their egg in order to hatch a pet. Once an owner has completed the required tasks, the egg will hatch and your solar pet will reveal. Our solar pets are broken up into four species. Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire and Amethyst. Emerald being the most common and Amethyst being the rarest. You will be able to feed, play, take care of, train and battle your pet. Each species has unique features and abilities as well as a unique and specific evolve form. Once your pet is trained to the desired level and a snapshot of Kalvin being in your wallet is taken, The Evo-V machine will become available. This allows you to evolve your pet into its corresponding evolve form increasing your pets rarity and abilities. Breeding will be available across all species and will provide many unique perks and abilities that will be announced in the near future!
